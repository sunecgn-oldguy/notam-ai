How it works
  1. Type the aerodromes in flight orderOne field: the first is your departure, the second your arrival, and everything after is enroute — alternates, tech stops, whatever you want briefed. IATA or ICAO, mixed freely: CPH BER LGAV EDDK. The line under the field always shows what the app understood.
  2. Select day, ETD and flight timePick Today or Tomorrow, then ETD (UTC) and EET. The briefing only keeps what is active in that window — wrong times, wrong NOTAMs.
  3. Save or edit your routesPick your airline, then a route, and the form fills itself. Press Edit to save what you have typed as a route, delete one, or add your own airline — flying for someone other than Star Air, build your own list. Everything stays on this device.

Every briefing you build is stored on the device and stays readable offline, in the air. Not for operational use — always cross-check your official briefing.
